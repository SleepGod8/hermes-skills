# Destiny 2 Guide

This skill covers how to assist Destiny 2 players with game advice — particularly **returning players** who need a catch-up plan, build recommendations, and progression roadmaps.

## ⚠️ Critical: Data Staleness

Destiny 2 is a **live-service game** with seasonal content cycles, power level (光等) squishes, and meta shifts. Your training data will be **out of date** for:
- Current light level caps (上限)
- Season/episode names (赛季名称)
- Current meta weapons and exotics
- Active expansions

**Strategy to handle this:**\n1. Ask the user directly: light level, what they last played, what season it is now.\n2. **NEVER guess absolute numbers.** Even "relative" advice based on guessed numbers is wrong. Say "我数据可能落伍了, 你告诉我当前实际光等和赛季名" then work from their info.\n3. Build advice on **general principles** (powerful vs pinnacle drops, soft vs hard caps) that don't change much between seasons.

## Returning Player (回坑) Progression Framework

### Phase 1: Catch-Up (软上限阶段)
- Until ~soft cap: **any drop increases light**
- Best sources: seasonal activity, Pale Heart Overthrow, public events
- Use this phase to also unlock subclass aspects/fragments

### Phase 2: Powerful (强力阶段)
- Powerful rewards (gold star markers on director)
- Rotators: Vanguard ×3, Crucible ×3, Gambit ×3
- Clan rewards
- Seasonal challenges

### Phase 3: Pinnacle (巅峰阶段)
- Only pinnacle-labeled activities increase light
- Nightfall (higher difficulties)
- Dungeon / Raid weekly clears
- Seasonal pinnacle rewards
- Hidden chests in Pale Heart

## Titan-Specific Advice

### Phase 0: Fresh Prismatic (No Aspects Unlocked Yet)

When a player just finished TFS campaign and unlocked Prismatic but has **zero aspects/fragments**:

| Slot | Recommendation | Why |
|------|---------------|-----|
| Super | **Twilight Arsenal** (虚空大斧) | Viable without any aspect synergy |
| Melee | **Frenzied Blade** (缚丝剑斩) | 3 charges, works out of the box |
| Grenade | **Pulse Grenade** (电脉冲雷) | Consistent AoE damage |
| Class Ability | **Thruster** (喷气冲刺) | Short cooldown, useful immediately |
| Exotic | **Precious Scars** (珍贵伤疤) | Heals on prismatic kills — survival for low-light |
| Fragments | **Facet of Protection** ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ | Damage reduction while surrounded |
|  | **Facet of Purpose** ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ | Picking up an orb gives matching overshield |

**Priority for this player:** Get the 1st aspect ASAP (do the Arrival quest chain in Pale Heart). Consecration should be #1 unlock.

### Prismatic Titan Aspect Priority
1. **Consecration** (炙烤锤击) — best for add clear + burst damage
2. **Knockout** (击倒) — sustain via melee kills
3. **Diamond Lance** (钻石冰矛) — crowd control
4. **Unbreakable** (坚不可摧) — survival for hard content
5. **Into the Fray** — strand-only synergy, low priority

### Recommended Exotics (回坑优先)
| Exotic | Use Case |
|--------|----------|
| Synthoceps | Melee damage + range, prismatic BiS |
| Precious Scars | Healing on prismatic kills, great for low-light |
| Cuirass of the Falling Star | Thundercrash nuke build |
| Hazardous Propulsion | Slide-rocket new exotic from TFS |

### Prismatic Titan Starter Build
- **Aspects:** Consecration + Knockout
- **Melee:** Frenzied Blade (triple strand charge)
- **Grenade:** Pulse Grenade
- **Super:** Twilight Arsenal (void axes) or Thundercrash
- **Class Ability:** Thruster (short cooldown, procs Knockout)
- **Exotic:** Synthoceps (strongest option)

## General Reminders for Returning Players

- **Lost Signal (失落的信号)** — Area Denial Frame stasis special GL from Pale Heart. Fires a lingering zone that slows/freezes. **Excellent pairing** with Prismatic Titan Consecration: drop zone → enemies clump → hammer. Get from Pale Heart activities/Overthrow.
- **Old gear is power-capped** — don't get attached to your old loadout for power
- **Destiny Content Vault (DCV)** — Mercury, Mars, Titan (moon), Io, Leviathan gone
- **Task log cleanup** — expired seasonal quests clog the log; delete them
- **Exotic Monument (凯旋纪念碑)** — at the Tower, spend Exotic Ciphers for old expansion exotics
- **Crafting** — some weapons craftable, newer ones may not be
- **Artifact mods** — check current season artifact and build around it
- **Power squishes happen** — don't be surprised if old 1600 is now 510
